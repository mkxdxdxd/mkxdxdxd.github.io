Research in 3D Computer Vision & Generative Models

I am a graduate student in the Kim Jaechul Graduate School of AI at KAIST, advised by Prof. Seungryong Kim. My research focuses on 3D vision, multi-view diffusion models, and geometry-aware generative models for novel view synthesis and 3D reconstruction. I am also working closely with Dr. Jin-Hwa Kim at NAVER AI Lab.

Broadly, I am interested in how we can endow generative models with a consistent understanding of the 3D world, bridging geometry, representation learning, and controllable generation.
